"I'll fight anybody for everybody."



Lore

Boru grew up in Tokyo, possibly in the split, as hinted by one of his dialogues. He is currently looking for a lost ancestor who might be connected to the samurai in Icebox.


Boru’s abilities were influenced by Ferrofluid, which is a liquid affected by magnetic poles. They are generally made of nanoscale ferromagnetic or ferrimagnetic particles suspended in a carrier fluid, generally an organic solvent or water.


Boru’s past and his future are interconnected with the mask.

COSMO






//Biography//

Type: CONTROLLER



Ghanaian Agent Cosmo harnesses the energies of the cosmos to reshape battlefields to her whim. With full command of her astral form and a talent for deep strategic foresight, she’s always eons ahead of her enemy’s next move.

"I am on a higher plane, chale. Literally"



Lore

Coming from Accra, Ghana, West-African agent Astra is a powerhouse of control. Astra is the 16th agent to join the LAFORANT Protocol and she is a Radiant with ties to the cosmos. Astra shows her care for her fellow agents through her jokes and serious concern while still focusing on the battle field. Astra's personality is bubbly and addictive while still being unique to herself.


Sliding into her Astral Form, Astra blocks out the noise of the battle field and can place her stars around the map. Her Nebula cloud provides coverage with style while her Nova Pulse and Gravity Well gives the enemy a concussion and keeps them there. The Cosmic Divide is a giant wall panning across the entire map which blocks sound and absorbs damage.


Special Abilities

Gravity Absorb

Create a Star to form a Gravity Absorb. Players in the area are pulled toward the center before it explodes, making all players still trapped inside vulnerable.


Darkness

Create a Star to transform it into a Nebula (smoke).


Cosmic Divider

Create an infinite Cosmic Divide connects the two points you select. Cosmic Divide blocks bullets and heavily dampens audio.


SASHA






//Biography//

Type: INTIATOR



“Hailing from Australia, Sasha and her band of beasts trail-blaze the way through hostile territory. With her creations hampering the enemy, and her power to heal others, the team is strongest and safest by Sasha's side.”

"Alright! Where's the fight?"



Lore

Sasha is a Radiant from Eastern Australia, defender of her homeland's flora and fauna. She is in her twenties and used to be a farmer, and some of her hobbies include working out in the gym, morning runs and wood working. She is from the town of Nimbin. Her calm and caring personality is complemented by the fact that she knows what it is to lose someone important.


She uses her Radiant powers to animate wooden figures which she calls her pack. The pack is her company, as she usually works alone. It consists of the wooden trinkets or totems as referred to in the game files: a hawk (more specifically, a brown goshawk), a Tasmanian tiger, and three creatures similar to jellyfishes. She also has a totem for her healing ability in the shape of four plants. Sasha has tried to create more complex animated animals, namely a dragon, which didn't turn out well.


Sasha has a strong connection with plants and animals demonstrated through her trinkets and the drawings on her suit. This has earned her the title of eco-warrior and shaped her antagonistic view towards the Kingdom Corporation and their invasion of natural spaces.


Sasha joined the LAFORANT Protocol after Sage invites her in. A Rift had appeared in the forest outside an unknown Kingdom facility in Australia. Sasha told Sage that she plans to stay and protect her land, but after a mention to many more Rifts all over the world, she accepted Sage's offer to become part of the LAFORANT Protocol. Once inside the Protocol, some of the agents believe that she has not developed her powers to their full potential.


Special Abilities

Guiding Light

Create a hawk trinket. FIRE to send it forward. HOLD FIRE to guide the hawk in the direction of your crosshair. RE-USE while the hawk is in flight to transform it into a flash that plays a hit confirm if an enemy was within range and line of sight.


Wolf-A

Create a Tasmanian tiger trinket. FIRE to send out and take control of the predator. While in control, FIRE to leap forward, exploding in a concussive blast and damaging directly hit enemies.


Seekers

Create a Seeker trinket. FIRE to send out three Seekers to track down the three closest enemies. If a Seeker reaches its target, it nearsights them.


Spye






//Biography//

Type: SENTINEL



The Moroccan information broker, Spye is a one-man surveillance network who keeps tabs on the enemy’s every move. No secret is safe. No maneuver goes unseen. Spye is always watching, becarefull.

"Nothing stays hidden from me. Nothing."



Lore

Spye, also known by his real name, Aamir, is a Moroccan tech user with a dark and mysterious past. He has no ties to anyone and seems to have lost his family in the past. Spye has a quirky sense of humor but is always watching the enemy.


Although he is a dangerous and dark person, Spye makes jokes and lightens up the atmosphere in the team. Many of his voice lines involve playing around with the rest of the LAFORANT Protocol, however Spye can also be much more morbid, referring to the enemy team in a dark manner. Spye also makes references to gambling, implying that he may have a history related to casinos. Spye's most sensitive aspect is when it comes to his late wife, Nora. He mentions also his family as a whole, and implies that he lost all or most of his family.


Spye uses information from his camera and Trapwires to take over the arena. Spye uses his self-made Spycam to tag enemies and reveal their locations, along with Spye-Cages to influence enemy movement. He uses tripwires to catch foes off guard, revealing their location and concussing them. Spye also has a hat which is more than it seems. When thrown at a corpse, it turns into a spider-like robot which reveals the locations of all living enemies around the map. Spye made his whole kit from the tripwires to his special hat.


Spye does not have proper relations with anyone, but he has a negative view towards Kingdom, as he thinks they take away the culture from places and tell lies about their practices. This may be because of their unethical and immoral practices, but Kingdom may have played a larger role in his past, possibly to do with his family.


Special Abilities

Spye Cage

Instantly toss a cyber cage in front of Spye. Activate from any distance to create a temporary zone that blocks vision.


Spye-Cam

Equip a spycam. Fire to place the spycam at the targeted location. REUSE this ability to take control of the camera's view. While in control of the camera, Fire to shoot a tracking dart. The dart is removable and will periodically reveal the location of the enemy hit. It can be destroyed by shooting it once with any gun. Disabled and revealed upon death.


Neural-Theft

Use on a fresh enemy corpse to throw Spye's hat. After a brief delay, all enemy players' locations will be revealed once.
